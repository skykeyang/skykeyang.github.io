CategoryHackathon
OrganizerCrypto.Com
Hackathon Date08 October, 2022

Project description

In 2022, I participated in the Crypto.Com: The Next Gen Hackathon together with a team of friends. We were given 3 problem statements to work on and we ultimately chose to tackle statement 2: "How we might use Web3 to solve a business/social/environmental problem.".

We made use of our Final Year Project's existing codebase and made several changes to better address the problem statement at hand. We also had to come up with a pitch deck for our solution which had to be created from scratch since the scope of the problem statement is vastly different from what we had to deal with in the final year project.
